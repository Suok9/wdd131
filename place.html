NIGERIA

About My Country

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is the most populous country on the continent. It has a rich history that dates back thousands of years, with evidence of early civilizations such as the Nok culture (around 1000 BC). The region later saw powerful kingdoms and empires like the Benin Empire, Oyo Empire, and Kanem-Bornu Empire. In the 19th century, British colonization led to the creation of modern Nigeria, which gained independence on October 1, 1960.
